In order to understand where "The Onceuh Experience" is going, I want you understand what it is.

ART
Noun: the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.


⛧ ⛧ ⛧

It's quite simple honestly, everybody has a different definition of art, for me, art is anything that can be produced visually from the creative mind.

Although I doubt many people would consider "edits" or "AMVs" a form of art, to me, thats art itself.
Before people get the wrong idea, I don't devalue Artists and Painters, bless them for their works of art.
"The Onceuh Experience" isn't to change the way people see art. It to recognize and appreciate another form of art.

My form of art.
